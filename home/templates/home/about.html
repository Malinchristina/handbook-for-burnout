My Journey and Why I Created This Library

The reason I wanted to create this library is that I myself went through a really tough burnout. The first few months were incredibly challenging; it could take me two hours just to get dressed for a walk, and I often ended up just sitting on the stairs outside my house. I had to set alarms to remind myself to eat breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Routines played a pivotal role in the beginning, and there was not much else I had energy for.

Eventually, as my nervous system began to recover, I wanted to add something more to my days, but I had no idea what. The creative part of my brain was totally shut down, so I started to google for ideas. Even that felt like too much effort. This library exists so that you don't have to go through that struggle. I want this to be a supportive and safe space where we can share our experiences.

No journey is the same, but we can all benefit from guidance from someone who truly understands where we're coming from.

If you have any feedback or questions, please feel free to send me an email.

Much love, Malin
